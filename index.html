Who's Cookie?...

Hi, I'm Cookie, a passionate graphic designer and illustrator situated in the vibrant vicinity of the Seattle Area. My creative journey is marked by many exciting projects, including the development of numerous comics and novels. Additionally, I'm thrilled to announce the recent inception of my very own fashion line, adding a stylish flair to my diverse portfolio. As I navigate through this dynamic world of visual storytelling, I find immense joy in collaborating with fellow creatives. Whether you're in need of a unique illustration, a captivating design, or someone to bring your ideas to life, I'm here to make it happen. If you're looking to elevate your creative endeavors, don't hesitate to reach out!
